About Me

Though born and raised in Ketchum, Idaho, I went to college in Oberlin, OH before moving back to the Pacific Northwest, landing in Portland in 2009. I enjoy playing music, working on/modding guitar gear, photography, road trips, hiking, biking, and going to the movies.
